===========================================
Welcome to China Tech Wiki!
===========================================

This a resource for tracking how Chinese technology companies impact the digital spaces as the country pushes to enforce its concept of "cyber sovereignty" and authoritarian regimes in other nations states follow suit. Areas covered here include censorship, the Great Firewall, Web3 and the metaverse.

The project is hosted on GitHub and is open to collaboration. Researchers, journalists and other interested parties can submit proposed changes through GitHub or flag inaccuracies by email_.
